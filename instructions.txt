Mushroom Omelette Recipe
1 Egg
1 Mushroom
1 Tspn of butter
1 pinch of salt
1 pinch of pepper

Method:
1. Whisk everything together
2. Add on low fire heat
3. Swirl egg around until coated entire pan
4. Let cook until slightly solid, then turn off heat and let cook on pan heat only
5. Serve with on plat with toasted bread and coffee
